Paris, France


Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world, known for its rich history, romantic atmosphere, iconic landmarks, art museums, and delicious cuisine. It attracts millions of tourists every year.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower - The most famous landmark in Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum - Home to the Mona Lisa painting.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral - A historic Gothic cathedral.
  4. Champs-Élysées - A popular shopping and entertainment street.
  5. Seine River Cruise - Enjoy breathtaking city views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant - A buttery French pastry.
  • Macarons - Colorful and delicious sweet treats.
  • French Onion Soup - A classic French dish.
  • Crepes - Thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Baguette - Traditional French bread.
